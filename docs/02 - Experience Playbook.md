# 📚 Experience Playbook

## Purpose

The Experience Playbook defines how every destination is designed.

It translates the creative vision into memorable moments.

This document answers:

"How should someone experience Singapore?"

---

# Experience Framework

Every destination should answer the following questions.

## Why this place?

Why is this destination essential to understanding Singapore?

---

## Emotion

What should the audience feel?

Examples:

- Wonder
- Curiosity
- Belonging
- Gratitude
- Peace

---

## Moment

What is the defining human moment?

Examples:

- Arrive
- Look Up
- Light
- Taste
- Pause
- Create
- Share
- Remember

---

## Value

Which Singaporean value is expressed?

Examples:

- Hospitality
- Harmony
- Community
- Determination
- Hope
- Belonging
- Resilience

---

## Interaction

What naturally happens here?

Never begin with trivia.

Begin with a moment.

---

## Discovery

What is the ONE unforgettable takeaway?

---

## Letter

What is Singapore gently sharing?

Never lecture.

Never boast.

---

## Memory

What keepsake does the audience receive?

---

## Atmosphere

What does the audience hear?

What do they see?

What do they feel?

---

## Cultural Touch

Which authentic Singaporean detail belongs here?

---

## Nostalgia Opportunity

Does this destination naturally allow nostalgic references?

If yes:

What are they?

---

## Implementation Notes

How will this eventually become code?
