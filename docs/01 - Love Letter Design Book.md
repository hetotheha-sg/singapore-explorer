# Chapter 0

# The Soul

## What is this?

An Interactive Love Letter from Singapore is a premium interactive digital experience where Singapore invites people to experience its stories, values, culture and everyday moments through meaningful interactions that become lasting memories.

It is not simply about visiting Singapore.

It is about experiencing Singapore.

Whether these places have been part of everyday life, or today marks a very first encounter, there are always stories waiting to be noticed.

---

## Vision

The experience should leave people thinking:

> "I never looked at Singapore this way."

or

> "I'd love to experience Singapore for myself."

Not:

> "I completed a game."

---

## The North Star

Singapore never asks anyone to be impressed.

Instead, it quietly shares a piece of itself.

The admiration is earned.

Never requested.

---

## Our Promise

Every feature should help someone experience a little piece of Singapore.

Not simply learn something.

Not simply complete something.

Experience something.
